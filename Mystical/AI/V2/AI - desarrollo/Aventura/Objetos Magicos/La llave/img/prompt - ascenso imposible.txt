Medieval mystery adventure illustration, realistic historical fantasy, adult audience.
Five exhausted medieval travelers cling to the side of an enormous vertical cliff hundreds of meters above the ground.
The rock wall is steep, smooth and intimidating, stretching far above and below them.
The men have no climbing equipment. They are trapped midway up the cliff with almost no handholds remaining.
Their expressions reveal fear, exhaustion and growing despair.
Some look upward searching for a route. Others glance downward, afraid to move.
The scale of the cliff makes the travelers appear tiny and vulnerable.
The scene should communicate vertigo, helplessness and the terrifying realization that they may all die on the mountainside.
