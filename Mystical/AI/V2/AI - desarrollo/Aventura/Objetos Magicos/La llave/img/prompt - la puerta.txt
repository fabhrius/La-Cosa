Medieval mystery and horror illustration, realistic historical fantasy, adult audience.

Deep inside a dark coastal cave at night, a lone medieval traveler stands holding an oil lamp.

The warm light illuminates rough stone walls and reveals an ancient iron door embedded directly into the rock.

The door is massive, plain and unsettling, with a single lock and no visible decorations, symbols or inscriptions.

The traveler stands motionless a few meters away, staring at the discovery in disbelief.

His clothes are worn, dusty and damaged from a long and difficult journey.

Beyond the circle of lamplight, the cave disappears into darkness.

The atmosphere should convey isolation, mystery and the feeling of finally reaching a destination after immense sacrifice.

No treasure is visible.

No supernatural effects.

The scene should feel realistic, ominous and suspenseful, as if the traveler has found something he has spent years searching for but does not yet understand.
