

The Temple of Rain, an immense ancient monolithic structure built from black volcanic stone, standing above a small medieval village on a storm-covered island. The architecture is colossal, mysterious and partially incomprehensible, created by a forgotten civilization thousands of years ago. Gigantic hollow pillars channel rainwater like vertical rivers through the structure. Endless stairways, eroded stone bridges, deep water channels carved into the floors, colossal weathered walls covered with moss and rain. A gigantic fractured tower rises above the temple, visible through fog and storms. On the main facade, an enormous eroded stone face almost erased by centuries of rain.
