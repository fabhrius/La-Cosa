For a long time,
Elara believed wisdom meant understanding everything.

The world.
The past.
Herself.

But the deeper she searched,
the larger the darkness became.

At first,
this terrified her.

Then she understood:

Perhaps intelligence was never meant
to illuminate the entire universe.

Only the next step.

And perhaps that was enough.



-------------------------------


Elara stood at the edge of the forest for a long time.

Beyond the trees,
the path vanished into darkness.

She tried to imagine every danger ahead.
Every possible mistake.
Every consequence.

But the future remained silent.

And slowly,
she began to understand:

The unknown was not blocking the path.

The unknown was the path.

Those who demand certainty before moving
never leave the safety of the fire.

So Elara stepped forward.

Not because she was unafraid.

But because she finally understood
that fear disappears only after the journey begins.
