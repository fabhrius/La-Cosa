Algo en su interior había despertado.
Necesitaba respuestas.
Creía que las encontraria al otro lado de las montanas.
Pero ignoraba que hubiera que pagar un precio.

La verdad no estaba oculta.
La esperaba,
 más allá de lo que estaba dispuesta a perder.


--------------------------


Something within her had awakened.
She needed answers.

She believed she would find them beyond the mountains.

But she did not yet understand
that a price would have to be paid.

The truth was not hidden.
It was waiting for her…

beyond what she was willing to lose.
