* teaching disciples,
* invoking mysterious forces,
* exploring ancient forests,
* standing before monumental engraved walls,

ruins, caves, temples, libraries, forests, mountains, underground chambers, forgotten sanctuaries, stormy skies, torchlight, candlelight, glowing inscriptions, arcane circles, and remnants of lost civilizations.
