Ancient underground library hidden beneath a colossal rain temple, dark fantasy realism. A narrow endless spiral staircase descending into absolute darkness, illuminated only by a single torch carried by an old priest in dark robes, followed by a young woman. The architecture is immense, ancient and monolithic, built from black volcanic stone by a forgotten civilization. Deep beneath the temple, gigantic underground chambers emerge from the shadows, filled with ancient books, scrolls, codices, maps, stone tablets and mysterious manuscripts stacked chaotically over stone tables and carved walls. Warm torchlight reveals only fragments of the immense library while most of the structure disappears into darkness. Atmosphere of forbidden knowledge, existential mystery, sacred silence, ancient wisdom hidden beneath the world. Cinematic composition, realistic textures, heavy shadows, fog, dust particles in the air, overwhelming scale, mysterious and unsettling mood, adult epic fantasy realism, highly detailed.
