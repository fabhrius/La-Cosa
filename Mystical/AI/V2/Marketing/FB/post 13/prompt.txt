Young female adventurer inside an ancient underground library, medieval fantasy setting. Elara stands beside a towering bookshelf filled with old books, scrolls, manuscripts, and forgotten records. She has just pulled an ancient book from the shelf and holds it carefully in her hands.

Focus on the contrast between the two characters:
Elara filled with optimism and expectation.
Khrom filled with caution and uncertainty.
