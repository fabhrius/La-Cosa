( Omniscientis — Fragment XV — The Temple of Rain — Part IX )
	Standing among endless shelves, Elara finally understood why the library defeated so many seekers.

The problem was not the absence of answers.
The problem was the abundance of them.

Every book opened a new path. 
Every answer gave birth to new questions.
No mind could examine every volume.
No life was long enough.
For a moment, the task seemed hopeless.
Then she remembered the words of Fabhrius.

Perhaps wisdom begins with choosing.
Deciding what to ignore.
Deciding what to pursue.

Perhaps, when possibilities become endless, 
the best strategy is not to search everywhere.

But to choose carefully where to look next.

(From “Omniscientis”, the lost codex of Lluviara)
