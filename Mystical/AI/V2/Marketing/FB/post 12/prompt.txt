
Epic fantasy scene inside an enormous underground library hidden beneath an ancient mountain temple.

A young female adventurer, Elara, stands on a stone balcony overlooking a vast abyss filled with bookshelves. She appears small compared to the immense structure around her.

Endless corridors disappear into darkness. Massive stone bridges connect distant galleries carved into the rock. Countless shelves packed with ancient books, scrolls, maps, manuscripts and forgotten records extend far beyond sight.

Warm lantern light illuminates parts of the library while the deepest sections vanish into mystery and shadow.

Atmosphere of wonder, discovery, mystery and ancient wisdom. The place should feel impossibly large, like an entire underground city dedicated to preserving knowledge.

Medieval fantasy setting. Ancient stone architecture. Highly detailed. Cinematic composition. Sense of scale is extremely important. Elara should be dwarfed by the library. Awe-inspiring, mysterious, majestic.
