Recovered from the ruins of Lluviara.

Where something had managed to survive
the erosion of time…

waiting to awaken once more.

— Omniscientis

Beyond the valleys of Lluviara,
buried beneath ruins older than memory,
the forgotten knowledge of a vanished world still endured.

Silent.

As if, for centuries,
it had been waiting…

for her.
