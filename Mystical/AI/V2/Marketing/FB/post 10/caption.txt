There are questions that never leave us.

Questions we carry for years.
Questions that return in moments of silence.

Some questions are simple.
Others can change the course of a life.

And then there are questions so profound...

that no answer seems enough.



Perhaps the most important question is the one you have not stopped asking.


(Omniscientis - The Temple of Rain - Part IV)
