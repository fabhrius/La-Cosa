

( Omniscientis — Fragment XIV - The Temple of Rain - Part VIII )

Not all paths can be walked.

Not all questions can be pursued.

For as possibilities multiply,
the horizon retreats beyond reach.

Thus the seeker faces an ancient law:

The world contains more doors
than any lifetime can open.

— Fabhrius

(From “Omniscientis”, the lost codex of Lluviara)




-----------------------------------------



For a long time,
Elara feared choosing.

Every path left another behind.

Every answer concealed a thousand others.

The world seemed too vast
for any life to understand.

Then she noticed something curious.

The wisest travelers did not know every road.

They simply learned
which signs were worth following.

Only then did she understand:

The purpose of intelligence
is not to explore every possibility.

It is to recognize
which possibilities matter.
